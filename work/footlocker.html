The Brief

Give These Designers a Real Platform

The Creative Collective NYC runs a program in partnership with Foot Locker, Footaction, and Champs called LEED that selects a cohort of emerging NYC fashion designers and gives them resources and visibility to grow.

We were hired across two rounds of production to create individual brand films for each selected designer and a short documentary capturing the full experience.

The Approach

Every Designer Gets Their Own Story

The challenge was balancing consistency with individuality. Each designer needed a film that reflected their specific brand voice while the documentary needed to capture the collective energy of the entire program.

We handled direction, camera operation, editing, and music supervision across both production rounds. One team, many voices, one cohesive body of work.

The Results

Content That Opens Doors

Each designer walked away with professional brand film content that could hold its own next to established brands. The documentary gave the CCNYC community a piece of work they could share and be proud of.

This is what community storytelling looks like when production quality matches the ambition of the people in front of the camera.
